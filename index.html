Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ReceiptAI, we collect: account info (name, email, password), expense records, receipt images for AI analysis, your country, and billing info handled by PayPal.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide ReceiptAI: authenticate you, store expenses, process receipts through AI, manage subscriptions, and send account emails. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • PayPal — all payment processing. We never store your payment info.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — receipt images sent for extraction, not retained beyond the API request.

4. Data Sharing & Sale

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share with service providers listed above.

5. Data Retention

Data retained while your account is active. Deletion requests processed within 30 days.
